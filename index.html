Police Shootings in U.S.A.

This map displays reports of police-related shootings in U.S. from the past few years (2011~2014). The data set is separated for each race in order to showcase comparison among the different races that are involved in police shootings. The data sets were crowdsourced from a popular website Deadspin. Each race is represented in a different color.

Please begin the observation by clicking and enabling a layer category from the menu on the top right corner of the map.
